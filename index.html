About Nasaeem Libya

Nasaem Libya for importing perfumes and cleaning materials is a leading company in the field of perfumes and hygiene in the State of Libya.

Based on more than a quarter of a century of experience in this field and on several commercial names, Nasaem Company was established Libya in 2015 to be the prominent name that aims to achieve the highest quality standards and provide the best products

  • Achieving customer satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Expand our business and presence in new markets.
  • Continuous work to develop new solutions and products that meet the aspirations of the market.
  • Actively participate in supporting social and environmental initiatives to achieve sustainability.
  • Continuous development of the company and keeping pace with the market Nasaem Libya Company for Importing Perfumes and Cleaning Materials.
  • Import and distribution of a variety of luxury perfumes.
  • Providing cleaning and body and skin care products.
  • Providing integrated distribution points that are spread throughout the country.
  • Planning to produce perfumes and cleaning materials locally.
  • Achieve long-term partnerships with both suppliers and distributors of products.
  • We also keep our list of services open looking ahead.
